Worldwide Days of Fasting and Prayer.

The first Sabbath of each quarter has been designated as a day of fasting and prayer for the world church by the General Conference of Seventh-day Adventists. We in Newlife are excited and eager to join our brothers and sisters around the world in praying for God’s Spirit. We know the end of the world is near. The job of sharing the three angels message is much too big for us. We need the indwelling of the Holy Spirit, personally and corporately. We invite everyone to join in these special days of fasting and prayer.

About Newlife

Newlife Seventh-day Adventist Church is a diverse Christian community comprised of individuals that seek to love God, each other and the greater community of Nairobi. Our church is a part of a worldwide community of Seventh-day Adventists. We share a love for Jesus and a commitment to demonstrating and spreading the good news of His transforming love to our neighborhoods, the city of Nairobi, and the world..

Our Mission

The Mission of the Seventh-day Adventist Church is to Proclaim to all the people the Everlasting Gospel in the context of the Three Angels Message of Revelation 14 : 6-12 leading them to accept Jesus as personal Saviour and to unite with His church, and nurturing them in preparation for His soon return.
