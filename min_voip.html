Our Ministries and Services

The Newlife Seventh-day Adventist Church Ministries and Departments

VOICE OF PROPHECY MINISTRIES

Newlife Sventh-day plans evangelism, and implements it through Church Voice of Prophecy Ministries' literature distribution, involvement in public evangelism organized by the Church in any place.

Elder : George Oyoo

Leader : Esther Maru

 

The Newlife Seventh-day Adventist Church Voice of Prophecy is a very vital office in the church. It is one of the foundational departments since the cradle of the church. It used to be a program to bring members into the church, thus it made a way for non-members to meet Jesus, learn much about Him, and become prepared for the end of the world and for the Second Advent of Jesus.

 

The Work of the Voice of Prophecy Office and Leader

 

1. The office plans evangelism, and implements it through V.O.P. literature distribution, involvement in public evangelism organized by the Church in any place.

2. It enrolls Church Members to be teachers, distributing agents, agents to enroll students into V.O.P. Discover Bible School.

3. Finds Friends who are interested in Bible studies and enroll them into V.O.P. lesson study guides.

5. Should work in closeness with Interest Coordinator to get the Interests and work on them.

6. Orders the lessons from the V.O.P. headquarters.

7. Receives the Lessons and distributes them to the agents for them to reach the right destinations.

8. Takes all V.O.P. records in:

 

i. All lessons.

ii. V.O.P. teachers and distributing agents.

iii. Students.

iv. New enrollees.

v. Marking done to the students lessons.

vi. Other V.O.P. records.

 

9. The leader and Chair of all the meetings and other V.O.P. Leaders.

10. Makes reports to the Pastor, Elders, Personal ministries Leader, other related departments and to the V.O.P. office in the Union.

11. Leads in the marking of the Students lessons and records them.

12. Prepare for the graduation ceremony for all Students who have finished the lessons.

13. Keeps follow-ups of all the Students who have graduated to make sure they have converted into Adventism.

14. Attends all the meetings of the Conference, Union, and others on V.O.P.

15. The officer is the member of the local Church board.

16. Makes the mission statement, vision, goals, and objectives, of the V.O.P. department.

17. Encourages Church Members to make good relationships with their neighbors, friends.

 

If you want to get involved with the Newlife Seventh-day Adventist Church’s Voive of Prophecy Ministries, or if you wish to talk with someone about learning more, please contact the church office during regular business hours at +254 (795) 638070 or by sending us an email.     Acts as steward of all the money contributed to the local church and the Central Kenya Conference of Seventh-day Adventist Church.

    Serve by assisting the Board of Elders in the care and nurturing of the church body.

  • The Adult Sabbath School

    Every week small groups get together to share what they learned from the lesson and how it has made a difference in their lives.

    Education Ministries of Newlife Church helps to educate our members and community on the importance of Christian education within the classroom, home, and extended families.

    Communications Ministries of Newlife Church aims to effectively communicate both the gospel message and the activities of the Newlife Church in a consistent, easy-to-understand fashion through a variety of mediums.

About Newlife

Newlife Seventh-day Adventist Church is a diverse Christian community comprised of individuals that seek to love God, each other and the greater community of Nairobi. Our church is a part of a worldwide community of Seventh-day Adventists. We share a love for Jesus and a commitment to demonstrating and spreading the good news of His transforming love to our neighborhoods, the city of Nairobi, and the world..

Our Mission

The Mission of the Seventh-day Adventist Church is to Proclaim to all the people the Everlasting Gospel in the context of the Three Angels Message of Revelation 14 : 6-12 leading them to accept Jesus as personal Saviour and to unite with His church, and nurturing them in preparation for His soon return.
