Pastoral message

"I am the vine; you are the branches."

Welcome to the online face of the Newlife SDA Church

  • By: Pastor D. Kateeti
  • Posted on: 17 November, 2016

You are warmly welcome to Newlife Seventh-Day Adventist church website. As part of God’s remnant church in this world, we are equally poised at maintaining our focus on Christ and His Second Coming. Even the whole world knows that our profession of keeping the commandment of God and hold the testimony of Jesus Christ throws us into the colosseum of truth leaving us increasingly vulnerable in this cosmic controversy. Surely, in this volatile spiritual contest that we have been called, torturing and tutoring it is yet there is still hope. Christ has promised that “all powers have been given to him”(Matt. 28:18,20) and also He is with us always until the end of time. This assurance is the spur we possess to enable us endure to the end, for our salvation (Matt. 24:13).

Are you seeking a church home? Do you question what value an institution like a church can possibly provide to our community today? This is a place where we encourage all to keep questioning. We welcome and cherish your heart, your mind, and your own growing ability to minister to the world. So take a look around! If you have any questions or comments, we’re happy to help answer them. Feel free to contact our pastoral staff, or better yet, drop by one Saturday morning and experience worship with us and you can talk to us in person. Either way, we pray you are blessed by your interaction with us.

Indisputably, every serious-minded member of the remnant church of God goes through a lot of challenges. Sometimes, they are so overburdened that people of God are driven down to ask “why does the righteous suffer?” Unfortunately, some even pass out yielding; others stand up even without answers to the question.

Friends, as the Lord’s Second Coming still tarries, 1 Peter 5:8,9 still warns us against the Devil’s snares, so there is the need to be sober and temperate for the Devil has not relented in his pursuit to get us destroyed. He did the same to the earlier saints; the scripture prompts us to resist him in our firm faith as we press on, knowing that this cosmic battle ends at the resurrection morning-Acts 14:22 also encourages us to endure to the end, for through many tribulations, shall we appear into the kingdom of God.

How could one endure in this world of meaninglessness? Friends, remember that;

1. By acknowledging Christ as Lord of the remnant church

2. By focusing on the risen Saviour, obeying his commandments and to live as he lived.

3. As disciples following the master wherever he goes and maintain our relationship with Him.

4. Finally, leaving anything that hinders us from being disciples whilst we make disciples ourselves.

I guess, with the above points under our sleeves, the remnants would always be revived and reformed to stand by the truth, hold the commandments of God and dare to be like Daniel.

Friends this message of encouragement is to help us press on while we still wait for “the blessed Hope”. I do not know the challenges and adversities that you are wadding through yet there is still hope. The spirit of prophecy encourages us that:

‘At all times and in all places, in all sorrows and in all afflictions, when the outlook seems dark and the future perplexing, and we feel helpless and alone, the Comforter will be sent in answer to the prayer of faith. Though circumstances may separate us from earthly friends, but no circumstance, no distance can separate us from the heavenly Comforter. Wherever we are, wherever we may go, He is always at our right hand to support, sustain, uphold and cheer us’. Desire of Ages pp. 669-670

Friends let these words of hope guide us as we get closer to probation, and in no time see His glorious face. On that note, l once again welcome you to this site.

Be Blessed Saints

About Newlife

Newlife Seventh-day Adventist Church is a diverse Christian community comprised of individuals that seek to love God, each other and the greater community of Nairobi. Our church is a part of a worldwide community of Seventh-day Adventists. We share a love for Jesus and a commitment to demonstrating and spreading the good news of His transforming love to our neighborhoods, the city of Nairobi, and the world..

Our Mission

The Mission of the Seventh-day Adventist Church is to Proclaim to all the people the Everlasting Gospel in the context of the Three Angels Message of Revelation 14 : 6-12 leading them to accept Jesus as personal Saviour and to unite with His church, and nurturing them in preparation for His soon return.
